HOW TO CROCHET A MINI-HEART

The following is a guide on how to crochet a small- heart shape

  1. Make a magic ring.
  2. Chain two, then make 3 treble crochets in the next three stitches.
  3. Make three double crochets in the next three stitches. Chain one, make one treble crochet, then chain one.
  4. Make three double crochets in the next three stitches.
  5. Make three treble crochets in the next three stitches.
  6. Pull through the yarn end from the magic ring to close the space, then slip stitch into the ring.

Now, you have a mini heart.
